Welcome To Snooker Club

Welcome to our premier snooker club, where cue sports enthusiasts come together to indulge in the art of precision and strategy. Experience camaraderie, refine your technique, and make your mark in the world of snooker at our club.

Reservation and Booking.

Experience seamless reservation and booking at our snooker club. Whether you're a casual player seeking a spontaneous game or a dedicated enthusiast planning ahead, our user-friendly online reservation system ensures you secure your preferred table with ease. Select your desired date and time, choose from our range of pristine snooker tables, and confirm your booking in just a few clicks. For our esteemed members, enjoy the added advantage of priority booking during peak hours. Walk-ins are also welcome, subject to availability. Embrace the thrill of precise gameplay without the hassle – reserve your spot now and cue up for an unforgettable snooker session.

Coaching Class

We provide different kinds of teaching class for each membership.

Gold Membership:

Gold members will have access to regular guided practice sessions led by experienced coaches. These sessions will focus on honing fundamental techniques, positioning strategies, and building consistency in shots.

Learn More

Diamond Membership:

Diamond members will receive personalized coaching plans tailored to their skill levels and goals. Our skilled coaches will analyze their gameplay, identify areas for improvement, and create a comprehensive plan to enhance their technique, break-building, safety play, and more.

Learn More

Platinum Membership:

Platinum members will have exclusive access to periodic masterclass workshops conducted by renowned snooker experts. These workshops will delve into advanced strategies, mental resilience, tournament preparation, and provide rare insights into the world of professional snooker.
